# PRODUCT.md — hanjo.ai

## What it is
hanjo.ai is the marketing site for hanjo, Kei Nakayama's bilingual (JP/EN) growth agency in Los Angeles. Four service lines: hanjo CRM (/crm/), hanjo Web (/web/), hanjo Ads (/ads/), hanjo Recovery (/recovery/). Every page is a single-file HTML document with inline CSS/JS and a data-i18n EN/JA dictionary.

## Register
brand

## Users
- JP-owned small business owners in LA (salons, head spas, estheticians, dental, clinics), Japanese-first readers, arriving from cold outreach or vivinavi. Skeptical of slick funnels; check 会社概要 and faces before replying.
- JP DTC/EC brand founders (Shopify) for the CRM line, bilingual.
- They read on phones, often via LINE or IG in-app browsers.

## Tone
Professional, calm, founder-personal. Direct claims backed by mechanics and proof, never hype. Japanese copy must read like a professional JP copywriter wrote it (no 直訳). Performance-based pricing ("成果報酬") is the trust anchor.

## Strategic principles
- Trust over flash: the site's job is to make a cold prospect believe a real, established operator is behind it.
- One page per service tells one straight story: problem → mechanism → cost logic → proof → free diagnostic.
- Bilingual parity: every visible string exists in EN and JA (/ja/ and /en/ path prefixes force language).

## Anti-references (Kei's explicit rejections, do not reintroduce)
- Anything "vibe-coded/AI-looking": pill badges, wavy underlines, em dashes, emoji, letterspaced JA kickers, awkward forced line breaks, decorative label dashes.
- 3D/heavy glassmorphism CTAs ("looks old", "hideous"); flat modern sage CTAs won.
- Times New Roman experiment (reverted; Playfair/Inter is the identity).
- Generic SaaS hero-metric templates and identical icon-card grids.
