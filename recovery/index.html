Common questions

What salon and clinic owners ask us first.

Is texting customers legal in California? Could I get sued?

Texting is legal when the customer has given consent, and consent is the only basis we ever text on. Every number opts in through your own form with clear, TCPA-compliant wording, and every message follows CTIA messaging guidelines with a one-tap "Reply STOP" that we honor immediately. We never buy lists and never cold-text anyone. That consent-first, TCPA & CTIA–aligned approach is exactly what keeps you protected under California and federal law.

How is my customers' data protected?

Customer contact details are encrypted, stored securely, and never sold or shared. We use them for a single purpose, helping that person complete their booking, and honor any deletion request in line with California's data-privacy law. You and your customers stay in control of the data at all times.

How do you track which bookings are yours to invoice?

Every SMS we send contains a unique tracked link. We only invoice when a customer clicks that specific link and completes a booking. Walk-ins, organic bookings, and repeat customers are never counted. You see the same log we do before approving any invoice.

What if it doesn't bring in any new customers?

You pay nothing. We absorb the setup cost and time. Our incentive is entirely aligned with yours: we make money when you make money. If it's not working, we keep optimising, or we walk away with no invoice.

Do I need to give you access to my ad accounts?

For ads management, yes: we request partner access to your Meta or Google account. You remain the owner. For the landing page and automation, no ad access is needed. You can start with just the funnel and add ads later.

What booking platforms do you work with?

We currently build for Square merchants. If you're on a different platform (Mindbody, Vagaro, Fresha, or anything else), let us know. We're open to developing for your setup so you don't have to make any drastic changes. We'll be upfront about what's possible before we start.

How long until the funnel is live?

Two weeks from kickoff. Day 1–3: audit and plan. Day 4–7: landing page built. Day 8–14: automation set up and tested. Day 14: live and tracking from day one.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No long-term lock-in. We ask for 90 days to see meaningful results, since funnels keep improving as we optimise. If you want to stop, just give us 30 days notice.
