Privacy Policy

When you contact us through our form, we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email address, company name, website, and the details of your inquiry. The Site also uses Google Analytics, which collects usage data through cookies.

2. How we use it

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry, prepare for consultations, and improve our services. We do not sell personal information, and we do not use it for purposes unrelated to your inquiry.

Form submissions are delivered to the internal tools we use to manage inquiries, such as Notion and Slack. Access is limited to hanjo.

4. Third-party services

The Site uses Google Analytics to understand how the Site is used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Google's handling of data is described in Google's own privacy policy.

5. Access and deletion

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time through our contact form. We will respond within a reasonable period.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is always available on this page.

7. Contact

For any questions about this policy, please reach us through the contact form.
